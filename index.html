Core

QR visitor & gate access

Residents pre-authorise visitors and contractors in seconds. The guard scans the QR at the gate — entry and exit are logged instantly, no paper book, no guesswork.

Popular

Maintenance & issue reporting

Residents report issues from the app; management triages and tracks every request from Open to Resolved — with status updates pushed back to the resident.

Incident & SOS alerts

One-tap panic and incident reporting route straight to on-duty security with live push — and every event is captured for the board's record.

Announcements & comms

Replace the noisy WhatsApp group with targeted announcements that land as push notifications — water lock-offs, gate closures, notices — all logged.

Visitor allowlist & passes

Keep recurring visitors, helpers and contractors on a pre-approved allowlist, and manage active passes — so regular faces get through without a fresh invite each time.

New

Remote gate opening

Guards open the gate straight from the security app — a tap pulses a Wi-Fi or GSM relay on your existing gate motor. No new gate to install, and every open is logged: who, which gate, when and why.

Maintenance-fee invoices and delinquency status sync from Zoho into the admin console, so management sees at a glance who's current and who's behind.

Admin dashboard & analytics

Audit trail & roles

Role-based access for admins, managers, guards and residents, plus an immutable audit log of who did what and when — accountability the board can rely on.

Secure & real-time by design

Personal data is encrypted at rest and updates arrive live over push & streaming, so residents, guards and management always see the same truth.

Questions, answered

Frequently asked

Do we need to install gate hardware?

No. Gatewise runs on the phones your residents and guards already have. There's nothing to wire into the gate — guards simply scan visitor QR codes with the security app.

What about visitors who don't have the app?

Visitors never need the app. They receive a secure QR pass by link or message; the guard scans it at the gate to verify and log the entry.

How do residents report a problem?

From the resident app they raise a maintenance request; management triages and updates the status, and the resident is pushed a notification as it moves from Open to Resolved.

Can management see who owes maintenance fees?

Yes. Invoices and delinquency status sync from Zoho into the admin dashboard, so management can see at a glance who's current and who's behind. (In-app card payments are on the roadmap.)

Is residents' personal data safe?

Yes. Personal information is encrypted at rest, access is restricted by role (admin, manager, guard, resident), and every action is recorded in a tamper-evident audit log.

How long does it take to go live?

Most communities are live within two weeks: we import your resident and lot list, set up your guards, and brand the apps for your estate.
